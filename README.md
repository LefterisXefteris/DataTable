# DataTable

DataTable is a comprehensive business management platform that consolidates all your operational needs into a single, intuitive spreadsheet-based application. Built with Next.js and modern web technologies, DataTable provides a familiar spreadsheet interface while offering the power and flexibility of a full-featured business management system.

## What is DataTable?

DataTable brings your entire business into one app with powerful spreadsheet management capabilities. Whether you're tracking inventory, managing orders, monitoring staff attendance, or tracking project progress, DataTable brings everything together in one unified workspace with a familiar Excel-like interface.

### Key Features

- **Real-Time Spreadsheet Interface**: Excel-like experience with instant updates and server-side persistence
- **Flexible Data Management**: Add, delete, and batch update rows with data protection and status tracking
- **Multiple Business Use Cases**: Inventory management, order tracking, staff management, project tracking, sales tracking, and asset management
- **Modern Tech Stack**: Built with React 19, Next.js 16, PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM, and Clerk authentication
